Magic Square

How to Play?

  • Click on a number and it will rotate the other numbers adjacent to it in a clockwise manner.
  • You have to set the numbers in such a way that the sum of the row, column and diagonal elements respectively are same.
  • Press the check button to see whether you got it or not.
  • Reset button resets the matrix to its original state.

Hint!

  • The middle element of any 3x3 magic square must be 5.
  • The magic sum is 15 ,i.e, the sum of each row,column and diagonal respectively is 15.
  • The pair of elements that fit in the corner, opposite to each other, are 2 and 8 , 6 and 4
